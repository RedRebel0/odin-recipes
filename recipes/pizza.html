Homemade Pizza

Description

A crispy, golden crust topped with zesty tomato sauce, melted mozzarella, and your favorite toppings. Perfect for a fun dinner at home!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Dried oregano and basil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread a thin layer of sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle cheese and toppings evenly.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes until the crust is brown and cheese is bubbly.
