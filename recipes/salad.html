Fresh Garden Salad

Description

A light and refreshing mix of crisp greens, crunchy vegetables, and a simple vinaigrette. It's the perfect side dish for any meal.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Olive oil and balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the lettuce and vegetables.
  2. Chop the lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Slice the tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions.
  4. Toss everything together in a large bowl.
  5. Drizzle with oil and vinegar just before serving.
