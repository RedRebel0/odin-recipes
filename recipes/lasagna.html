Description

This is a classic meat lasagna recipe with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and bubbly cheese. It is the ultimate comfort food for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Meat sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles until al dente.
  2. Brown the meat and add sauce ingredients.
  3. Layer noodles, cheese, and sauce in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 375Â°F for 45 minutes.
  5. Let cool before serving.
